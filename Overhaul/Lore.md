The world of Fathoms is a world ruled by fickle tides. In this vast ocean, many toil within a grand pattern too large to comprehend – that unfathomable order called *fate*. In this land of shifting islands, of lunar dragons and creatures from the depths, there stand few who can defy their ordained path.

Through the power of the *Hand*, Heroes, Mages, and Buccaneers plunder fate to serve their own agendas. Through their actions, the fate that rules Fathom is thrust into chaos.

*But what is chaos, if not an order too vast to comprehend?*
