A player character is made out of a combination of various different things: 
- Race
- Background
- Archetypes
- Formative Events
- Skills

Race and Background determine the archetype options and statistics of a character. They can also provide skills.

Archetypes are the primary progression pathway and the source of most direct combat abilities. 

Formative events are separate from background and provide roleplaying pathways as well as skills.
