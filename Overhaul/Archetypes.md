## Heroic Archetypes
### Knight
The knight is the juggernaut of the battlefield, bearing an invincible defence and a blistering offence. This archetype uses a limited supply of breakthrough points to fuel offensive and defensive manoeuvres. 
- Bulwark
- Shield Bearer
- Meteor

### Soldier
Soldiers are the backbone of any organised offence, acting in unison with allies to turn the tides of battle. This archetype uses an array of strategies to adapt to and control the flow of battle.
- Vanguard
- Executioner
- Skirmisher

### Opportunist
Opportunists are perceptive fighters who do not hesitate to ruthlessly exploit a foe's weaknesses. This archetype selects a specific target and strikes with lethal intent to end the battle in a single moment.
- Shadow
- Duellist
- Spy

## Magical Archetypes

### Sorcerer
Sorcerers are spellcasters who learn the magical arts through natural means, rather than rigorous study. This archetype can cast a never-ending deluge of spells, but is less versatile than other casters.
- Druid
- Elementalist
- Sage

### Wizard
Wizards master magic through the study of magic itself, learning to cast spells 'the right way' – although, that is just what a wizard would claim. This archetype has access to an array of powerful and versatile spells, but can only prepare a limited number of spells to cast.
- Scholar
- Diviner
- Inscriber

### Spellslinger
Spellslingers are the true battle spellcasters, using various mediums to cast efficient and practical spells. This archetype has powerful and unlimited spells, but requires setup time in battle to reach its full potential.
- Artillerist
- Conductor
- Smoke Dancer

### Saint
Saints draw on the light deep within all to empower their spellcasting. This archetype has comparatively weak spells, but access to rare healing magic as well as the ability to enhance magic with the user's own health and willpower. 
- Caretaker
- Afflicted
- Inquisitor

### Occultist
Occultists draw their power from the image of a divine beast, casting magic that matches their mighty patrons. This archetype has access to many unique branches of magic, including some which may fray away at the user's will.
- Seeker
- Contractor
- Eldritch Scholar

### Necromancer
Necromancers possess nigh-limitless potential, having the ability to resurrect the corpses of their foes and fallen allies to fight alongside them. This archetype focuses on reanimating and controlling zombies rather than direct spellcasting, being able to create minions of varying power depending on which bodies are available.
- Horde Lord
- Artificer
- Lich Ascendant

### Oracle
Oracles tap into the very weave of fate itself, learning to see and touch the forces behind reality. This archetype focuses on manipulating game mechanics and card draws to its advantage.
- Fate-Twister
- Gambler
- Chronomancer
