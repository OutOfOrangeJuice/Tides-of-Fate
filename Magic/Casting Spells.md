Spellcasters have access to all their spells at any point. However, to actually cast spells, you need to use cards from your spellcasting hand. Different classes have different methods for filling the hand, and different spells will require different cards to cast.

When the active deck is reshuffled, casters lose all their hands so that the deck can be reshuffled in full. However, spellcasters can then draw cards back equal to what they lost.

##### Cantrips
Spellcasters have a number of cantrip slots dependent on their level. Every time you level up, you designate which of your spells become cantrips. A spell takes up a number of cantrip slots equal to how many actions it takes to cast (for spells with variable casting duration, select a specified duration which will be used every time the cantrip is cast).

Cantrips can only be cast on your turn, but take no actions to cast. However, you may only cast one cantrip per combat round.
