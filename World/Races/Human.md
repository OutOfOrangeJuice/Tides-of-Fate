Stats: +3 to one stat of choice, +2 to aptitude, +1 to moxie

Humans are the most populous race in the world being the majority population on most settled islands and fortresses. While not particularly good at any one thing, humans are known for their versatility as well as for the sheer breadth of skills and advancements among their communities. 
Humans look like humans and have human features.

#### Features
Walk Speed: 30ft
Swim Speed: 15ft
Languages: Common
