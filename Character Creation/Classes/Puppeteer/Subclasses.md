#### Master of Strings
This subclass doubles down into the base class abilities, allowing you to puppet more enemies and allies as well as do more to disrupt or enhance their abilities.

#### Puppet Fighter
This subclass grants a construct which the puppeteer can control to fight alongside him. The construct can specialise in a variety of martial archetypes. Similar to an animal companion, but you control it on your turn – it's more like a weapon rather than a separate entity.

#### Ritualist
A spellcasting subclass for the puppeteer. The Ritualust casts spells through traps. The spells it can cast are limited, but with the right preparation a ritualist can be a devastating force on the battlefield.

#### Doll Summoner
The Doll Summoner specialises in creating large numbers of weaker constructs to control and summoning them magically onto the battlefield to take advantage of vulnerabilities. Dolls can have various abilities, but the abilities are limited and the dolls are temporary. In exchange, you lose the ability to puppet enemies. 

#### Shadow Manipulator
This subclass focuses more on the sinister aspects of the class, giving abilities that allow you to do more evil things to your puppeted enemies. Shadow manipulator has a focus on causing chaos in enemy ranks, so it's better if your dm is willing to rp in combat.
